MANIFEST NO. 02 — ABOUT THE HOUSE

A trading house built on the paper trail
as much as the produce.

Sarthi Exim India is a merchant-exporter working directly with growers and processing units across India to bring six core spice and produce lines to international buyers. Every consignment moves with full documentation — quality certificates, phytosanitary papers, and commercial invoices — so it clears customs without surprises.

Mission

Give overseas buyers a dependable, well-documented source of Indian spices — the kind of supplier you don't have to double-check.

Vision

To be the first call for B2B spice buyers across the Gulf, Europe and Southeast Asia looking for consistent grade and on-time shipping.

Company Timeline

  1. Y1Founded in Mira Road, Maharashtra, sourcing red chilli and turmeric for domestic trade.
  2. Y2First export consignment cleared IEC and shipped to a Gulf buyer.
  3. Y3Added pepper, cardamom, garlic and onion lines; registered with Spices Board.
  4. Y4Built recurring buyer relationships across multiple export corridors.
  5. TodayOperating as a full-line merchant exporter with in-house documentation and QC.
